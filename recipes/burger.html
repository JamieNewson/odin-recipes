Burger

The meanest, keenest, toughest burger in town, right from your kitchen.

Discover our best burger recipes, with everything you'll ever need to put inside a bun, from juicy beef patties, to chicken, turkey, veggie and vegan burgers.

Ingredients

  • Patty
  • Bun
  • Lettuce have Lettuce

Instructions

  1. Grind that fatty patty into a paste, grind cinammon sticks and turn.
  2. Spread the juicy goodness around a pan & cook on high heat for 10 minutes.
  3. Once bronzed, place into bun with lettuce & serve.
